Floorplanner

AI-powered floor plan editor with OCR

Product Overview

Floorplanner is the internal admin headquarters for Trukeys, designed to convert static floor plan images into the interactive property catalogs showcased on the platform. By combining browser-based computer vision (OCR) with a sophisticated 2D canvas editor, it enables internal teams to rapidly digitize complex layouts and catalog precise area metrics.

Major Product Decisions

Vision AI for Instant Room Extraction

Instead of forcing users to draw every room from scratch, I integrated Sarvam AI's vision models. Users simply upload a floor plan image, and the system automatically identifies rooms, labels, and dimensions, populating a "Room Palette" for quick placement.

Interactive Canvas with Real-Time Metrics

I decided to build the editor using Konva.js to ensure high-performance interactions like drag-and-drop and grid snapping. Crucially, the system calculates area metrics in real-time, providing instant feedback on total square footage as rooms are rearranged.

Market Differentiation

Optimized for Internal Administrative Control

Unlike consumer-facing CAD tools, Floorplanner is an internal powerhouse meant for Trukeys administrators. It prioritizes data integrity and rapid catalog generation, ensuring that every property on Trukeys is backed by clean, verified floor plan data.

Smart Gap & Overlap Detection

Unlike generic drawing tools, Floorplanner understands the "logic" of a floor plan. It features intelligent gap detection to ensure rooms are perfectly aligned and provides suggestions for optimal layout density.
