Product Overview

TruKeys is a real estate platform built on the foundation of trust and transparency. In a market often clouded by aggressive sales tactics and biased information, TruKeys provides a buyer-centric alternative. It offers verifiable builder insights, connects real buyers to share experiences, and enables informed property decisions—all while strictly enforcing a no-spam user experience.

Major Product Decisions

Strict "No-Spam" Policy

The biggest pain point in Indian real estate is the deluge of marketing calls once a user registers. I made the core product decision to keep user contact details private, even from builders, until the user explicitly requests an interaction. This builds immediate trust and differentiates us from lead-gen engines.

Community-Driven Feature Prioritization

To maintain radical transparency, I implemented a public feature voting system. Users can suggest and vote on platform features, showing that the product's direction is directly shaped by the buyers it serves.

Market Differentiation

Buyer-Centric vs. Broker-Centric

While major platforms (99acres, Magicbricks) are designed to serve the brokers paying for leads, TruKeys is built solely for the buyer. Our loyalty lies with the user's decision quality, not the builder's sales volume.

Builder Trustworthiness Scores

We developed a unique scoring model for builders based on historical delivery timelines, legal compliance, and actual buyer satisfaction, providing a level of objective insight that traditional platforms avoid to save their advertiser relationships.
