Product Overview

GlintPost is a B2B SaaS tool designed to bridge the communication gap between product teams and their users. It provides lightweight, embeddable widgets for changelogs and roadmaps, allowing companies to showcase their progress and collect feature requests directly within their own applications. By focusing on "Product Marketing," GlintPost helps teams turn every update into an engagement opportunity.

Major Product Decisions

Embeddable Widgets vs. Standalone Portals

Instead of forcing users to visit a separate subdomain, I decided to prioritize an "embed-first" strategy. This keeps the user within the product context, significantly increasing the visibility of updates and the likelihood of feedback collection.

AI-First Feedback Management

Traditional roadmaps often suffer from "feedback noise"—dozens of duplicate suggestions. I integrated an AI-powered duplicate detection system that proactively identifies and merges similar feature requests, saving hours of manual moderation.

Market Differentiation

Targeted Change Delivery

Unlike generic changelogs (e.g., Beamer, Headway), GlintPost allows for content delivery based on specific visitor attributes. This means beta features can be shown only to beta testers, and enterprise-specific updates only to enterprise users.

Deep Visitor Analytics

Most competitors offer basic view counts. GlintPost tracks deep engagement metrics, showing exactly which user segments find specific features most interesting, enabling data-driven product marketing.
