Product Overview

AgentLane is a comprehensive, multi-tenant SaaS CRM purpose-built for the overseas education industry. Recognizing the unique complexities of student recruitment—long conversion cycles, massive document requirements, and multi-stage university applications—AgentLane provides a streamlined workspace that manages the entire lifecycle from initial lead to final enrollment.

Major Product Decisions

Dual-Pipeline Architecture

Most CRMs have a single lead/deal flow. I decided to build two distinct but connected pipelines: one for "Leads" (pre-eligibility) and one for "Students" (post-selection). This prevents the student funnel from being cluttered with unqualified leads and allows for specialized automation at each stage.

Visual 'If-This-Then-That' Automation

Instead of complex scripting, I implemented a visual rule builder. Consultants can easily set up automations like "if a student is stagnant for 5 days, reassign to senior counselor," empowering non-technical team leads to optimize their own workflows.

Market Differentiation

Vertical-Specific Focus

Unlike generic tools like Salesforce or HubSpot, AgentLane understands the "Education Domain." This means built-in fields for IELTS scores, visa statuses, and university application tracking, eliminating months of custom configuration effort.

Integrated 6-Step Onboarding

I designed a guided wizard that takes organizations from signup to fully operational in minutes. It handles team invitation, pipeline configuration, and automation setup in a logical sequence, drastically reducing churn during the setup phase.
