A B2B SaaS tool for product teams to communicate updates and collect feedback through embeddable widgets. Features AI-powered duplicate detection, multi-locale support (8+ languages), targeted content delivery, and visitor analytics.

**India's most trusted home buying platform** · [trukeys.com](https://trukeys.com)

A real estate platform focused on transparent home buying with unbiased builder insights, community-driven feature voting, and no spam calls.

### AgentLane CRM
**Modern CRM for overseas education consultants**

A purpose-built CRM featuring dual pipeline management (leads & students), a visual automation rule builder, drag-and-drop task management, marketing campaign tools, and role-based team collaboration.

An intelligent floor plan editor that uses Sarvam AI to extract room dimensions from uploaded images. Features an interactive drag-and-drop canvas, smart layout suggestions, real-time metrics, and grid snapping tools.

**Stack:** Next.js · TypeScript · Konva.js · Sarvam AI · Supabase · Tesseract.js
